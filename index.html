Fantasy Game

Inklyn

Inklyn is a whispering scribe who breathes life into forgotten memories. In this text-driven fantasy world, you run a mystical sanctum where you craft scrolls of joy, courage, or even precognition. Serve quirky patrons, venture into AI-generated narrative nodes, and engage in strategic turn-based "memory duels." Every word you write and every choice you make unravels the mystery behind your uncle's disappearance and thwarts an ancient plot to erase the town's memories.

Space Survival

Volyra

Volyra is a space survival text-based adventure game. Stranded with your AI companion aboard a failing deep space research station, you must issue commands through a limited interface to stay alive. Combine verbs and nouns to build instructions, manage energy, oxygen, and system integrity, and make critical decisions within a 45-minute countdown. Every word you speak could mean life or death, and every choice leads to one of six distinct endings.
