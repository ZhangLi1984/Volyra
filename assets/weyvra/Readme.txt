Weyvra - Weave Word 



It is a word driven fantasy narrative adventure. You embody a weaving spirit journeying through a world where meaning has shattered. Type words to define reality reframe chaos and resonate emotion repairing corrupted anchors of sense. Collect lost civilization lexemes and evolve your vocabulary into potent forces. In this realm built entirely of text every word is a thread mending the cosmos your language is the fire of creation.
